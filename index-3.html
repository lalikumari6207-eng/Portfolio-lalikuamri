Who I Am

About Me

I am Lali Kumari, Founder & CEO of Lyzonix Technologies and Sanjhex.

I am from Bihar, India, and have completed a B.Sc in Chemistry — a background that gave me strong analytical thinking and an eye for detail I now apply to building products and brands.

I am a visionary entrepreneur focused on building scalable digital solutions and impactful brands. I don't just follow trends — I create them. With a strong foundation in technology and business, I work on developing innovative IT solutions that help businesses grow faster and smarter, while also leading the creation of Sanjhex, a unisex streetwear brand designed to redefine modern youth fashion.

I believe in execution over ideas, consistency over shortcuts, and building something that creates real value. My mission is to establish Lyzonix as a powerful multi-service company and launch products that stand out in competitive markets.

I don't just follow trends — I create them.

— LALI KUMARI, FOUNDER & CEO
